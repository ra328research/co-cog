Why it matters

Changes in dementia care needs can emerge between routine appointments. Co-Cog is intended to support earlier recognition of changes and help services prioritise support.

Current status

Co-Cog has been co-produced with people with lived experience, carers, health and care staff, voluntary sector representatives, and technology partners. Further research is planned to refine how it could fit into real-world care pathways.

For more information, please contact the Co-Cog research team.
